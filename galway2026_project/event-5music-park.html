Music in the Park

Live music performances in Salthill Park.

Activity Description

Enjoy live music performances in a scenic outdoor park setting, featuring Irish traditional music, indie bands, and contemporary artists.

  • Evening outdoor concerts
  • Food and beverage vendors onsite
